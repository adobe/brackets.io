  2. harish

    What are the keyboard shortcuts for the following?

    1) switching between split view and normal view.
    2) split from vertical to horizontal
    3) switch between left and right panes.

    This would be of great help.

    Thanks.

    Reply
    • Peter Flynn
      October 9, 2014 at 7:19 pm

      #1 and #2 can be addressed by assigning keyboard shortcuts to the View menu items. Currently the easiest way to do that is via the Key Remapper extension, but in the very next release of Brackets we’ll have a simpler built-in way to do this.

      For #3, you can use Ctrl-Tab to navigate between recent documents, including documents in the other pane. You can also use Quick Open to quickly switch to any document (open or not) by typing just a few letters from its name. There is no shortcut dedicated to switching panes, though. See issue 9036 for discussion of adding that.
